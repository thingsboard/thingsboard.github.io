Introduction<\/h4>\n\n\n\n

For solar farms, dirty panels can mean thousands of dollars of lost revenue annually. Panels washed too early or too late \u2013 are costs that make up a serious portion of the power plant\u2019s profitability.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

In one of our cases, we worked with a Spanish company, a producer of electricity. They owned a solar power plant with a capacity of 10 MW. They will produce about $260,000 worth of energy per month in ideal conditions. However, the company lost over a fifth of its energy due to soiling problems. In turn, the cost of the cleaning cycle was $50,000. Calculating the losses, it was clear that cleaning on a fixed schedule was showing poor results.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
