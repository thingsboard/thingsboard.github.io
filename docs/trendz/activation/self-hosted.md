This section describes how to activate Trendz for the ThingsBoard Self-hosted environment via the License Portal.

## Activation Steps

### Step 1. Log in to the License Portal

* Open the ThingsBoard License Portal.
* Sign in with your TB account as sysadmin.

[screenshot: License Portal login]

### Step 2. Select Your Instance

* In the list of licensed installations, choose the TB instance you want to enable Trendz for.

[screenshot: list of instances]

### Step 3. Open Add-ons

* Inside the instance profile, navigate to the Manage Add-ons tab.
* Enable checkbox in Trendz analytics.
* Click “Next” in the lower right corner.

[screenshot: Add-ons tab]

### Step 4. Review Summary

On the Summary screen you will see:
* Selected plan
* Full price
* Click “Create Subscription”.

[screenshot: summary page]

### Step 5. Complete Payment

You will be redirected to the payment step.

Buttons:  
Go to License Portal (link to the LP)  
Pricing (modal window with pricing)  
Installations guide (link)

Full documentation is available in the main navigation menu.
